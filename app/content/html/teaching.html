Outreach Activities

My outreach activities range from developing educational videos and training school teachers to working closely with policymakers to inform the disaster risk reduction decision-making process and strengthen the resulting policies. Some of my activities are highlighted below.

  • since 2021 - Voices for Science (Policy track), American Geophysical Union (AGU)
  • since 2020 - Member of the European Geosciences Union (EGU) Outreach Committee
  • since 2020 - Member of PhD/Postdoc Representatives, Geosciences, University of Tübingen
  • 2019 - Science adviser, European Union Parliament, Brussels, Belgium
  • 2019 - UN Delegate, Forum on Science, Technology and Innovation, NY, USA Read more
  • since 2011 - Founder of ParsQuake Project
  • since 2008 - Curriculum design, teacher professional development and education research
