Public Engagement & Outreach

Through outreach activities, we aim to raise awareness of the challenges of communicating scientific uncertainties to audiences with varying levels of scientific literacy in order to promote a transparent dialogue between science and society. Some of our recent activities are highlighted below.

  • Tübingen Science & Innovation Days 2024 Read more, We organized a series of activities and events as part of the Tübingen Science & Innovation Days 2024, which have the motto “Besser streiten” (argue better). The aim was to raise awareness of the challenges of visualizing uncertainties and promote a transparent dialogue between science and society. Activities included an interactive eye-tracking station, a World Cafe discussion depicting uncertainties in graphics, and a panel discussion exploring scientific data in everyday visual communication.(Read more)
  • (forthcoming) Tübingen Stadtmuseum 2026 - In partnership with the Stadtmuseum Tübingen, we are currently teaching a master-level, Service-Learning course at the University of Tübingen titled 'Exploring Uncertainties: Fostering Civic Engagament on Natural Hazards in Tübingen'. Students' final project in form of museum exhibits will be displayed for public viewing and interaction in February 2026 at the Stadtmuseum Tübingen.
